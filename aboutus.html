About Us

Mission Statement:

At Fenote Training Academy, our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills necessary to save lives and thrive in healthcare and public health settings. Through accredited, high-quality, hands-on training, we provide services that meet the evolving demands of the industry. We are dedicated to fostering professional growth, ensuring excellence in patient care, and enhancing the health and safety of communities through education and consultancy.

In partnership with the National Phlebotomy Association, National Public Health Association, American Medical Technologists, and the National Healthcareer Association, we offer accredited training programs in Medical Laboratory Assistant, Phlebotomy, and Pharmacy Technician.


Additionally, through collaborations with the American Health and Safety Institute (HSI), American Red Cross (ARC), and The American Heart Association (AHA), we provide accredited certification programs in CPR, First Aid, BLS, and Instructor Training, ensuring individuals are fully equipped with life-saving skills to make a meaningful impact in their careers and communities.
