Mission Statement
At Fenote Training Academy, we equip individuals with accredited, hands-on training in CPR, First Aid, BLS, Phlebotomy, Child Care, and Community Health. Certified by the Maryland Department of Health and Education, ARC, HSI, and the National Phlebotomy Association, our programs support excellence in healthcare and public safety. Beyond training, we offer national public health consultancy and lead global health projects, especially in Kenya, Uganda, South Africa, and India, working to strengthen healthcare systems and uplift communities worldwide.
Additionally, through collaborations with the American Health and Safety Institute (HSI), American Red Cross (ARC), and The American Heart Association (AHA), we provide accredited certification programs in CPR, First Aid, BLS, and Instructor Training, ensuring individuals are fully equipped with life-saving skills to make a meaningful impact in their careers and communities.
