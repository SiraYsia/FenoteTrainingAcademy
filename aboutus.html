About Us

OUR MISSION IS TO SAVE LIVES!

Fenote Training academy is in partnership with American Health and Safety Institute (HSI), American Red Cross (ARC) and the American Heart Association (AHA) with the mission to spread knowledge and educate as many members as possible of the national community in lifesaving procedures. Our services include training courses for CPR and AED, First Aid, BLS (Basic Life Support), Babysitting, and more. Whether you are looking for a required certificate for work or finding new ways to support and serve those around you, Fenote training academy with the American Red Cross and the American Heart association can help.

Fenote Training Academy is a leading provider of emergency preparedness products and solutions. As a licensed provider of American Red Cross training courses and compliance programs, our commitment to community health and safety is at the core of our mission.


Established with a focus on delivering high-quality training, Fenote Training Academy recognizes the vital role of community health and safety programs. Our journey began with a vision in 2023, aiming to address the growing need for comprehensive training services.


Aligning with the principles of the American Red Cross, Fenote Training Academy is dedicated to serving communities in need. By offering essential safety products and award-winning certification training courses in CPR, First Aid, AED, Basic Life Support, we strive to contribute to the well-being of individuals and communities.
