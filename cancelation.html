RESCHEDULING POLICY

To reschedule, contact us at ftrainingacademy12@hotmail.com, providing proof of attempted communication. Rescheduling is allowed once per course within 90 days of the original class date. You may only reschedule once for each course you registered for. The rescheduled class must take place within 30 days of the original class date.

Rescheduling notice:

  • 72 hours in advance or more: No fee; one reschedule permitted.
  • 24-48 hours before the course: 24-48 hours before the day of the course: If you contact us within 24 hours to 48 hours before the start time of your class, an additional $25 fee will apply for all classes. .
  • 24 hours or less: Rescheduling not allowed; considered a NO SHOW (refer to NO SHOW policy).
  • Rescheduling more than once is not permitted, except for extreme weather conditions resulting in MTA or DOE suspensions.
  • If you wish to reschedule the course for a second time, and if the administrative team considers and approves your reason for extension, you will be required to pay 50% of the price.
