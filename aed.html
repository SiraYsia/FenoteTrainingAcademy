AED

In addition to obtaining a CPR Certification, the enrollment and successful completion of the CPR and AED course, encompass the proper utilization of an AED (Automated External Defibrillator). Further training materials and certification options are available for child and infant CPR and AED training. Guidelines set forth by the American Red Cross and American Heart Association and other national organizations differentiate between CPR and AED use for adults, children, and infants. These distinctions will be thoroughly explained during your training course and CPR AED education. Supplemental guidelines from the American Heart Association are accessible online, in print, and in digital formats.


Upon finishing our CPR AED training and receiving your certificate, you may choose to advance your knowledge with Fenote Training Academy by enrolling in additional courses. This allows you to enhance your skills, pursue advanced certification training, and even explore the possibility of becoming an instructor. As an instructor, you gain access to the latest course materials and our online instructor portal, providing current information, training techniques, and emergency procedures. Your ability to save lives is extended through the students you train.


Our certification is globally recognized and valid across the world. Whether you're an everyday individual or someone whose job requires a CPR and AED training certification, our training course is designed to cater to your needs. In addition to the AED and CPR training, we recommend and often see participants taking the First Aid class prior to the CPR and AED courses. Fenote Training Academy also provides First Aid training. The First Aid Certification complements the knowledge and training received through CPR and AED, ensuring individuals are well-prepared to potentially save a life. Similar to blended learning and online CPR AED, the First Aid component includes an online prerequisite. Acquiring education in First Aid CPR AED for adults, children, and infants forms a cornerstone of a world-class skill set for life-saving.


We acknowledge and accommodate individuals with special requirements for online and practical exam administration. Recognizing that issues may arise during online exams, Fenote Training Academy prioritizes confidentiality, and students are encouraged to contact us with confidence for prompt assistance. All interactions with Fenote Training Academy and shared information are treated with strict confidentiality.
