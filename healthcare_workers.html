HEALTHCARE WORKERS

Healthcare professionals in VA, DC, and MD are mandated to renew their Basic Life Support (BLS) certification every two years. At Fenote Training Academy, our BLS courses are designed to make the learning experience engaging by incorporating various scenarios and delving into different CPR techniques in each course cycle.

Advanced Training Equipment

Participants receive hands-on training using state-of-the-art equipment that provides real-time feedback, ensuring proficiency in these crucial, life-saving skills.

Comprehensive Certification

Additionally, our comprehensive First Aid/CPR/AED course includes valuable training, and we offer a bundled package with the BLS course for a more comprehensive certification.

On-Site Training Available

We bring the training to you! Fenote Training Academy extends its services beyond its classrooms. We can conduct group classes at your facility, utilizing your on-site equipment and facilities as part of the training. This ensures that personnel become familiar with their specific resources, enhancing the overall learning experience.
