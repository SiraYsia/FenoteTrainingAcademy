CARDIOPULMONARY RESUSCITATION

In the course of a lifetime, individuals may witness over a thousand significant accidents. Among these incidents, some may occur where you could potentially be the first responder. Basic training in CPR and First Aid can be the decisive factor in saving both another person's life and your own. Swift action is crucial, as a person in need of CPR must receive treatment within 3 minutes to prevent severe neurological damage.

Immediate Response Matters

Waiting for healthcare providers might not be an option, and someone on the scene must perform CPR. Millions of non-medical professionals and ordinary individuals acquire CPR and AED skills to assist until EMT/911 assistance arrives, making a significant impact on life and death situations. Fenote Training Academy adheres to guidelines set by the American Red Cros, Health & Safety Institute, and American Heart Association, OSHA, and Emergency Cardiovascular Care standards.

Professional Instruction

Our team of certified instructors readies individuals to handle emergency or trauma situations. Information and training materials from professional emergency responders and organizations like the American Red Cross and American Heart Association provide insights into appropriate responses during emergencies, potentially saving lives.

Certification Process

CPR, or Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation, should be a fundamental skill for everyone, refreshed regularly through courses. Performing CPR on-site where an incident occurs is essential. CPR AED certifications are not merely convenient skills with certifications but critical life-saving skills.
