Medical Laboratory Assistant Training

Our Medical Laboratory Assistant Training combines online learning with in-person practice. You'll gain essential lab skills like specimen handling and phlebotomy through interactive online modules and hands-on sessions. This flexible course prepares you for real-world lab work with practical experience.

Phlebotomy Training

Acquire the skills needed for Phlebotomy. Prepare for the National Phlebotomy Exam and future careers with Fenote Training Academy's accelerated phlebotomy course, combining flexible online learning and hands-on in-person training by expert instructors.

CPR

Master the life-saving skill of Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR). Our courses teach the proper techniques to perform CPR and assist individuals experiencing cardiac emergencies. Training for Adults, children and infants.

First Aid

Learn essential first aid techniques. Be prepared to provide immediate assistance in emergency situations. Our courses cover a range of scenarios to ensure you're ready to act when needed. Training for Adults, children and infants.

AED

Understand the use of Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs). Gain the skills to respond effectively in sudden cardiac arrest situations. Our training ensures you can confidently use AEDs to save lives. Training for Adults, children and infants.

Basic Life Support (BLS)

Acquire the skills needed for Basic Life Support (BLS). Our training program covers essential techniques to support individuals experiencing life-threatening emergencies, including CPR and more. Training for Adults, children and infants.

First Aid, CPR, AED Instructor Training

Our First Aid, CPR, and AED Instructor Class is designed to equip you with the skills to teach these essential courses. This training is provided by Fenote Training Academy through the American Red Cross and will prepare you to deliver high-quality instruction in life-saving techniques.

BLS Instructor Training

Certification program for individuals to become instructors in Basic Life Support (BLS) training.

Mobile Phlebotomy Services

Mobile phlebotomy services provide blood draws and health assessments at locations such as homes, offices, event spaces, skilled nursing facilities, and research organizations. These services are performed by trained mobile phlebotomists, bringing laboratory testing directly to you.

Public Health Consultancy Services

Expert advice and strategic planning on public health initiatives, program development, and health policy to improve community health outcomes.
