Training Courses

First Aid

Learn essential first aid techniques. Be prepared to provide immediate assistance in emergency situations. Our courses cover a range of scenarios to ensure you're ready to act when needed. Training available for Adults, children and infants.

CPR

Master the life-saving skill of Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation (CPR). Our courses teach the proper techniques to perform CPR and assist individuals experiencing cardiac emergencies. Training available for Adults, children and infants.

AED

Understand the use of Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs). Gain the skills to respond effectively in sudden cardiac arrest situations. Our training ensures you can confidently use AEDs to save lives. Training available for Adults, children and infants.

Basic Life Support (BLS)

Acquire the skills needed for Basic Life Support (BLS). Our training program covers essential techniques to support individuals experiencing life-threatening emergencies, including CPR and more. Training available for Adults, children and infants.
