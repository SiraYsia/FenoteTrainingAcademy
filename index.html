Medical Laboratory Assistant Training

Learn essential lab skills, including specimen handling and phlebotomy, through a blend of online and in-person training. Gain practical experience for real-world lab work.

Phlebotomy Training

Prepare for a phlebotomy career with a mix of online and hands-on training. Learn blood collection techniques essential for medical tests, transfusions, and research.

Billing Training

Master medical coding and billing with our self-paced online course. Learn to apply ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS codes, manage claims, and ensure compliance with healthcare regulations. Prepare for certifications like CPC or CBCS with interactive materials and expert support.

Pharmacy Technician Training

Prepare for a pharmacy technician career with our online program. Learn pharmacology, medication dispensing, prescription processing, and customer service. Includes interactive lessons and real-world simulations for certification exams like PTCB.

CPR

Learn life-saving CPR techniques for adults, children, and infants, preparing you to assist in cardiac emergencies.

First Aid

Master first aid skills to provide immediate assistance in emergencies. Training covers various scenarios for all ages.

AED

Gain confidence using Automated External Defibrillators (AEDs) to respond to sudden cardiac arrests.

Basic Life Support (BLS)

Learn essential techniques, including CPR, to support individuals in life-threatening emergencies. Training for all ages.

First Aid, CPR, AED Instructor Training

Get certified to teach First Aid, CPR, and AED courses through Fenote Training Academy's American Red Cross program.

BLS Instructor Training

Become a certified instructor in Basic Life Support (BLS).

Mobile Phlebotomy Services

We provide mobile phlebotomy and health assessments at homes, offices, and other locations for convenient, on-site testing.

Public Health Consultancy Services

Receive expert guidance on public health strategies, program development, and policy planning to improve community health.
