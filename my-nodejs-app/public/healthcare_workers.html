BASIC LIFE SUPPORT COURSES

Health Care Workers

Healthcare professionals in VA, DC, and MD are mandated to renew their Basic Life Support (BLS) certification every two years. At Fenote Training Academy, our BLS courses are designed to make the learning experience engaging by incorporating various scenarios and delving into different CPR techniques in each course cycle. Participants receive hands-on training using state-of-the-art equipment that provides real-time feedback, ensuring proficiency in these crucial, life-saving skills.

Additionally, our comprehensive First Aid/CPR/AED course includes valuable training, and we offer a bundled package with the BLS course for a more comprehensive certification.

We bring the training to you! Fenote Training Academy extends its services beyond its classrooms. We can conduct group classes at your facility, utilizing your on-site equipment and facilities as part of the training. This ensures that personnel become familiar with their specific resources, enhancing the overall learning experience.

STOP THE BLEED

Enroll in our free Stop the Bleed course led by experienced EMS personnel. Learn essential life-saving interventions and bleeding control methods.

This course was developed in response to the U.S. Department of Homeland Security and the Hartford Consensus, aiming to enhance survival rates during active shooter and mass casualty events.

Emergency Medical Responder: In critical situations, bystanders play a crucial role. Gain knowledge about scene safety, various bleeding types, applying pressure, and using a tourniquet.
