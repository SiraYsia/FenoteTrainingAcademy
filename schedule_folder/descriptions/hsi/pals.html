ASHI Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS)

The ASHI Pediatric Advanced Life Support (ASHI PALS™) program is a CAPCE-approved, scenario (case-based) continuing education course intended for trained and skilled healthcare providers who either direct or participate in cardiopulmonary and resuscitation emergencies in children. Based on the official text of the program, PALS Study Guide, 4th Edition by Barbara J Aehlert RN BSPA, ASHI PALS provides students with an opportunity to enhance and refine their knowledge and hands-on skill proficiency in emergency cardiovascular care.

Intended audience

Emergency personnel in intensive care or critical care departments and emergency medical providers.

Participant pre-requisites

All individuals who participate in an ASHI PALS class must be currently trained, skilled and be able to:

  • Perform high-quality pediatric basic life support (PBLS)
  • Read and interpret basic electrocardiograms (ECGs)
  • Understand basic resuscitation pharmacology, prepare and administer essential medications
