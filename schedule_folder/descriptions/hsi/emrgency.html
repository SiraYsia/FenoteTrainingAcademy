Emergency and Fire Preparedness

This lesson raises awareness about fire and emergency dangers, outlines the requirements for emergency action and fire prevention plans, and highlights best practices for responding to alarms and practicing preparedness through drills. It does not address hazardous waste material emergencies or response procedures such as cleanup. This online-only course does not include in-person skills. Participants who successfully complete the online portion will receive a Certificate of Online Training. Length: 30 min
