Pediatric First Aid

This class focuses on the knowledge needed for first aid care in a pediatric setting. This program conforms to the 2015 AHA and ARC Guidelines Update for First Aid.

Course Topics Include

  • Role of the pediatric first aid provider
  • Recognizing an emergency
  • Legal considerations
  • Choking
  • Emergency moves
  • Primary assessment
  • Control of bleeding
  • Shock
  • Head, neck, or back injury
  • Burns
  • Poisoning
  • Environmental emergencies
  • Bites and stings
