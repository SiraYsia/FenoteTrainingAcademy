Phlebotomy Training Overview

Fenote Training Academy is a licensed and accredited organization offering a comprehensive phlebotomy course. This program prepares students for both the role of a phlebotomist and the National Phlebotomy Exam. Our curriculum blends online and in-person instruction to ensure a thorough learning experience.

What is Phlebotomy?

Phlebotomy involves drawing blood from patients for medical testing, transfusions, research, or donations. It is crucial for diagnosing and monitoring health conditions.

Why Phlebotomy Training?

Training is essential for acquiring the skills needed to safely and efficiently collect blood samples. It covers techniques, patient interaction, safety protocols, and legal aspects.

Function of Phlebotomy

The main function is to collect blood specimens for laboratory analysis, aiding in disease diagnosis, condition monitoring, and blood donation.

The Role of a Phlebotomist

  • Preparing patients for blood collection
  • Performing venipuncture
  • Ensuring correct sample labeling
  • Maintaining patient comfort and safety
  • Following infection control protocols
  • Collaborating with laboratory personnel

Online Training

Clinical Externship

Fenote Training Academy does not guarantee externships. A learner's placement in and completion of an externship is solely the learner’s responsibility and may be subject to additional requirements, such as a background check and successful completion of Fenote Training Academy course. However, Fenote Training Academy will assist you in finding externships and provide recommendations for opportunities

Exam Preparation

We offer an online preparatory exam to test your understanding before the National Phlebotomy Exam. This practice exam simulates actual test conditions to help build confidence and ensure readiness.

Requirements

Applicants must be 18 or older and hold a high school diploma.
