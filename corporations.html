STOP THE BLEED

Enroll in our free Stop the Bleed course led by experienced EMS personnel. Learn essential life-saving interventions and bleeding control methods.

This course was developed in response to the U.S. Department of Homeland Security and the Hartford Consensus, aiming to enhance survival rates during active shooter and mass casualty events.

Emergency Medical Responder: In critical situations, bystanders play a crucial role. Gain knowledge about scene safety, various bleeding types, applying pressure, and using a tourniquet.
