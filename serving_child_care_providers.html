Training Modules

Module 1: Child Development (CDV)

4 Hours
This training equips childcare providers with essential knowledge and strategies to support children's overall development. By understanding milestones, exploring developmental theories, implementing holistic strategies, and recognizing the role of play, participants will be empowered to create nurturing and effective learning environments for children.

Module 2: Curriculum (CUR)

4 Hours
This training will focus on two key areas: designing developmentally appropriate curricula and maintaining safe, healthy environments that support children's well-being and learning. Participants will acquire skills to foster children's growth and development effectively.

Module 3: Health, Safety & Nutrition (HSN)

4 Hours
Participants will learn essential components of creating a safe and healthy childcare environment. This includes recognizing signs of illness, emergency preparedness, and effective meal planning, empowering providers to support children's health and safety.

Module 4: Special Needs (SPN)

4 Hours
This training provides insights into typical and atypical developmental patterns, ADA compliance, and inclusive practices to support all children. Participants will be equipped to create supportive environments for children with diverse needs.

Module 5: Professionalism (PRO)

4 Hours
Focused on self-employed childcare business essentials, this training covers MSDE compliance, contract and policy development, and basic business practices, providing tools for operating a successful and compliant childcare business.

Module 6: Community (COM)

4 Hours
This training emphasizes building family partnerships and community ties. Participants will learn about cultural influences, community resources, and strategies to create a cohesive, supportive early childhood environment.


Registration Details

Each core knowledge training will take place over two days each month, with each session lasting 2 hours. Quizzes will be administered after each session, and a final assessment will be conducted during the final week.
