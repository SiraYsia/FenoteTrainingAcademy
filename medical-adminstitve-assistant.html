Medical Administrative Assistant Training Program

Ready to kickstart a rewarding career as a Medical Administrative Assistant? Our comprehensive course is designed to equip you with the essential skills to excel in a medical office environment. You will learn to manage electronic health records, facilitate effective communication with patients and staff, and ensure the smooth operation of daily office tasks. This program prepares you for the Certified Medical Administrative Assistant (CMAA) exam, setting you up for success in a respected healthcare role.

What You'll Learn

  • Computer Basics: Gain proficiency in the software necessary for organizing information, creating documents, and effective communication.
  • Healthcare Fundamentals: Understand basic anatomy, medical terminology, and the legal and ethical aspects of healthcare.
  • Office & Record Management: Master daily office operations, patient scheduling, payment processing, and health record maintenance.
  • Patient Care Coordination: Learn best practices for administering and coordinating patient care and education.
  • Digital Technology: Develop a solid understanding of digital security, internet usage, and essential software applications.
  • Communication & Customer Service: Enhance your communication skills, focusing on verbal, non-verbal, and written techniques vital for patient interaction.
  • Medical Laws and Ethics: Explore key legal and ethical issues related to medical practice, including documentation and medication administration.

Industry Demand

With the rapid growth of healthcare services, the demand for medical administrative assistants is strong, with projected job growth of 8% by 2032, making it a stable and promising career.
