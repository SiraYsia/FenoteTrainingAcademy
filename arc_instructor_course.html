ARC First Aid, CPR, AED Instructor Class

At Fenote Training Academy, our First Aid, CPR, and AED Instructor Course is designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to teach these essential life-saving techniques. This bleneded course will prepare you to instruct the American Red Cross's First Aid, CPR, and AED programs. To enroll in this instructor course, participants must hold a current CPR/AED certification (or a higher level) from the American Red Cross. Additionally, participants must be at least 16 years old by the final day of the course. The training spans approximately 5.5 hours, ensuring a comprehensive learning experience. Upon registering, you will receive access to the online portion of the course materials within 24 hours. Please note that fees may apply for appointment cancellations or rescheduling.
