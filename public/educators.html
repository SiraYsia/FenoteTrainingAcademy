EDUCATORS

Achieve certification in emergency intervention, earn Continuing Education Units (CEUs), and stay compliant with our American Red Cross-certified Adult and Pediatric First Aid/CPR/AED course. Aligned with mandatory CEU requirements nationwide, our engaging curriculum features hands-on training (mandatory in VA) and real-life scenarios, providing participants with specific education tailored for the educational environment. We consistently update our curriculum, ensuring each class brings variety and insights, transforming recertification into an opportunity to acquire new and vital information.

We extend our instruction to corporate groups, assisting in regulatory compliance or enhancing emergency preparedness, with available group discounts that may also contribute to lower insurance premiums.

We bring the training to you!Fenote Training Academy doesn't just offer courses in its classrooms; we also come to your school to conduct group classes. This includes utilizing on-site equipment and facilities as part of the training, allowing educators to be familiar with their specific resources.
