Indviduals

Ensure OSHA and state compliance with our group classes and discounted group rates.

Equip yourself with essential life-saving skills through our Individuals training program. Achieve certification in emergency intervention, earn Continuing Education Units (CEUs), and stay prepared with our American Red Cross-certified Adult and Pediatric First Aid/CPR/AED course.

Our engaging curriculum is designed to meet national certification requirements, featuring hands-on training and real-life scenarios. Stay up-to-date with the latest information as we consistently update our curriculum, turning recertification into an opportunity to acquire new and vital knowledge.

Whether you're an individual seeking certification or part of a corporate group looking to enhance emergency preparedness, our courses offer flexibility and valuable insights. Group discounts are available, contributing to lower insurance premiums. Additionally, Fenote Training Academy brings the training to you! Our on-site classes utilize your resources, ensuring familiarity with specific environments and equipment. Join us to empower yourself with the skills needed in critical situations.
