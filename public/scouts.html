SCOUTS

Wilderness Exploration is a comprehensive 4-hour course provided by Fenote Training Academy, offering essential skills for survival in the great outdoors, including locating food, establishing shelter, and navigating through the wilderness.

Fenote Training Academy presents this fundamental Wilderness Exploration Class, delving into introductory topics for safe venturing and exploration in a scouting environment. This program serves as an initial introduction for those aspiring to join Cub Scouts, Boy Scouts, Eagle Scouts, STEM Scouts, and advanced programs facilitated by the Boy Scouts of America (BSA). Embracing the ethos of becoming the very best future selves, youth members seeking membership and certification in Cub Scouting, Girl Scouts, Boy Scouts, Scouts BSA, and Scout Law can embark on their scouting journey with foundational knowledge gained from this introductory course. Spanning from Cub Scouts to Sea Scouting and reaching Eagle Scouts, each scouting association takes pride in offering leadership skills and fundamental wilderness venturing programs to prepare young individuals. Throughout the journey, young adults will develop physically robust skills, explore moral choices, and nurture self-esteem.
