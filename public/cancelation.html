CANCELLATION AND REFUND POLICY

  • Refunds will not be issued once registration is processed.
  • In the event of a class cancellation, full credits are available for up to 90 days from the original class date, subject to associated fees.
  • Cancellation requests within 24 to 48 hours before the class are subject to fees (refer to Rescheduling Policy). No cancellations, reschedules, or refunds are honored within 24 hours of the class date/time.
  • Fenote Training Academy reserves the right to cancel classes due to natural disasters, extreme weather conditions, MTA suspension, building shutdowns, or other uncontrollable causes. In such cases, rescheduling without penalty or holding credit without expiration is allowed.

RESCHEDULING POLICY

To reschedule, contact us at ftrainingacademy21@gmail.com, providing proof of attempted communication. Rescheduling is allowed once per course within 90 days of the original class date.

Rescheduling notice:

  • 72 hours in advance or more: No fee; one reschedule permitted.
  • 24-48 hours before the course: $25 fee for all classes.
  • 24 hours or less: Rescheduling not allowed; considered a NO SHOW (refer to NO SHOW policy).

Rescheduling more than once is not permitted, except for extreme weather conditions resulting in MTA or DOE suspensions.

LATENESS POLICY

Arrive 15 minutes before class. If more than 15 minutes late, entry is denied. Rescheduling is allowed for lateness of 15 to 45 minutes, as per the "24-48 hours before the day of the course" policy.

NO SHOW POLICY

A NO SHOW student is one who does not attend class without 24-hour notice. NO SHOW students are ineligible for refunds, credits, or rescheduling.

BLENDED COURSES

Blended Courses require completion of an online course before the scheduled class date. Failure to do so results in non-attendance for the skills session without refund or rescheduling.

REPLACEMENT CARD/MISPRINTED CARD

To replace a certification card, contact ftrainingacademy21@gmail.com. Pay the replacement card fee for name misspellings.

E-LEARNING/ONLINE COURSES

No refunds after registration. Exchanging from online to in-class is not permitted. Refunds granted only for the skills session, not the online part.

PAYMENT POLICY

Accepted: ACH, Elctronic Check, Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express.

CREDIT CARD CHARGEBACK/DISPUTE

Disputed charges incur a $25 processing fee, in addition to the original charges.

PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is important. Information is used within Fenote Training Academy. No credit card information is stored. Partners have access for transaction completion.

LIABILITY POLICY
